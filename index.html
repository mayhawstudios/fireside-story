It was a dark and stormy night...

A man in a trenchcoat walks down an alley when he encounters a stray cat... The cat meows.

The man goes to pet the stray cat. Suddenly, the cat hisses and pulls out a laser pointer!

The cat blasts the laser pointer into the man's eyes, temporarily blinding the man. The cat dashes off with the man's wallet.

The man staggers down the alley and runs into a trash can, and a stray raccoon falls out of the trash.
